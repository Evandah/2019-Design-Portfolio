Safety for Surfers

The Challenge

Surfers rarely get swept out to sea, but things can go terribly wrong if they do. Our challenge was to help surfers prepare an easy to use rescue plan for those dangerous situation.

The process

  • Brand Identity
  • Mobile App UI/UX design
  • Product Positioning

Digitising surf preparation

My strategy involved working with surfers to understand their process to prepare for a dangerous surf, then digitising that experience into a user friendly app.

The end result was an app called "Backup plan". An app that will automatically detect when you are at your favourite surf locations, then with one tap of the finger - the surfer can program their phone to message the coast guard if they are not back within a given amount of time. This involved a lot of story mapping to make sure we design for every scenario.

Product design direction

Surfers enjoy the adrenaline rush and danger of the unknown. We understood there is nothing sexy about preparation - so before we designed any high fidelity user flows I defined the brand position and user tested a marketing campaign.

This approach allowed us to define the product design direction, and ensure the messaging is correct.

Behind the scenes

Designing to a budget

To cut down on cost, I researched into native mobile UI components that we could use. Designing with this approach removed 8 screens with another 8 screens using pre-built native components.
